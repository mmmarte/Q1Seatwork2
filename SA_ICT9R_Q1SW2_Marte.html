Noli Me Tangere Characters

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin		 The son of Rafael Ibarra. A kind and well-mannered man. He stayed and studied in Europe for seven years.

Rafael Ibbara		 The father of Crisostomo Ibarra. He was accused of not going to confessions and for killing someone without any witness. He died while jailed.

Kapitan Tiago		 A friend of Rafael Ibarra. He hosted a party at his place. He is said to be generous or open handed.

Father Damaso		 A Fransiscan priest. He is emotional and likes control. He is said to have someone unburied and buried somewhere else.

Tenyente Guevarra		 He is the one who told most of the information to Crisostomo Ibarra about his fathers death. He tried to find attorneys that could defend Rafael Ibarra.
